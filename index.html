About ENSTA

The National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) was established by Executive Decree No. 22-314 of September 12, 2022. It replaces the former National School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences in Algiers, inheriting their assets and staff. As a public institution with scientific, cultural, and professional status, ENSTA provides advanced education, research, and technological development in fields such as industrial engineering, transport, automation, electronics, telecommunications, mechanics, electrotechnics, and process engineering. Since the 2023–2024 academic year, it has also added the highly sought-after Mathematics and Computer Science (MI) program. ENSTA stands as a center of excellence, offering top-tier training to support the nation's economic and industrial sectors.

MI Specialties

Artificial Intelligence Specialty

The Artificial Intelligence specialty at the Higher National School of Advanced Technologies in Algiers provides students with a strong foundation in mathematics, computer science, and data analysis. It focuses on developing intelligent systems capable of learning, reasoning, and adapting to complex environments. Through courses in machine learning, neural networks, and natural language processing, students gain both theoretical understanding and practical skills to design innovative AI solutions.

Graduates of this program are equipped to contribute to cutting-edge fields such as robotics, autonomous systems, data science, and intelligent decision-making. The specialty prepares future engineers to drive digital transformation across various sectors and to address real-world challenges using the power of artificial intelligence.

Cybersecurity Specialty

The Cybersecurity specialty trains students to protect digital systems, networks, and data from evolving cyber threats. It combines technical knowledge in cryptography, network security, and ethical hacking with strategic understanding of risk management and digital defense. Students learn to detect vulnerabilities, design secure architectures, and ensure the integrity and confidentiality of information systems.

Graduates emerge as highly skilled cybersecurity engineers capable of safeguarding national infrastructures, corporate systems, and emerging technologies. This specialty prepares them to play a crucial role in maintaining trust and resilience in the digital era.
